# Wool Sneaker E-commerce Site - Design Style Guide

## Design Philosophy

### Core Concept: "Natural Wool Meets Urban Tech"
The design embodies the intersection of natural luxury and modern functionality. Every element should evoke the softness, warmth, and authenticity of natural wool while maintaining the clean, sophisticated aesthetic of urban technology.

### Visual Language
- **Emotional Tone**: Calm confidence, premium quality, sustainable luxury
- **Brand Personality**: Sophisticated yet approachable, eco-conscious without being preachy, modern with natural roots
- **User Feeling**: Wrapped in comfort, confident in quality, connected to nature

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors (Low Saturation Warm Tones)
- **Warm White**: #F8F6F2 (primary background, clean and soft)
- **Oat**: #E8E0D6 (secondary backgrounds, gentle warmth)
- **Mist Grey**: #D4C8BC (accents, subtle depth)
- **Soft Taupe**: #C4B5A0 (text emphasis, natural grounding)

### Accent Colors
- **Sage Green**: #A8B5A0 (sustainability indicators, low saturation)
- **Warm Charcoal**: #4A453E (primary text, strong contrast)
- **Cream**: #FEFCF8 (highlights, pure softness)

## Typography

### Primary Font: "Tiempos Headline" (Serif Display)
- **Usage**: Hero headlines, brand name, section titles
- **Characteristics**: Elegant, editorial, premium feel
- **Weights**: Light (300), Regular (400), Medium (500)

### Secondary Font: "Suisse Int'l" (Sans-serif Body)
- **Usage**: Body text, navigation, product descriptions
- **Characteristics**: Clean, modern, highly readable
- **Weights**: Light (300), Regular (400), Medium (500)

### Font Hierarchy
- **H1 (Hero)**: Tiempos Headline, 4.5rem, Light
- **H2 (Section)**: Tiempos Headline, 3rem, Regular
- **H3 (Subsection)**: Suisse Int'l, 1.5rem, Medium
- **Body**: Suisse Int'l, 1rem, Regular
- **Caption**: Suisse Int'l, 0.875rem, Light

## Visual Effects & Styling

### Background Effects
- **Primary**: Subtle wool texture overlay with soft gradients
- **Secondary**: Organic flowing shapes in mist-grey tones
- **Accent**: Gentle particle effects mimicking wool fibers

### Animation Library Usage
- **Anime.js**: Smooth scroll reveals, button hover states, cart animations
- **Splitting.js**: Text reveal animations for headlines
- **ECharts.js**: Sustainability metrics visualization (if needed)

### Hover Effects
- **Buttons**: Gentle lift with soft shadow expansion
- **Product Cards**: Subtle scale with warm glow overlay
- **Images**: Ken Burns effect with overlay text reveal
- **Navigation**: Underline animation in sage green

### Scroll Motion
- **Reveal Timing**: Elements appear when 30% visible
- **Animation Duration**: 300ms ease-out transitions
- **Stagger**: 100ms delay between grouped elements
- **Movement**: Maximum 20px vertical translation

## Layout & Spacing

### Grid System
- **Container**: Max-width 1200px, centered
- **Columns**: 12-column CSS Grid with 24px gutters
- **Breakpoints**: Mobile (320px), Tablet (768px), Desktop (1024px)

### Spacing Scale
- **XS**: 8px (tight elements)
- **SM**: 16px (component padding)
- **MD**: 32px (section spacing)
- **LG**: 64px (major sections)
- **XL**: 128px (page sections)

## Component Styling

### Buttons
- **Primary**: Warm charcoal background, cream text, 12px border-radius
- **Secondary**: Oat background, warm charcoal text, 2px border
- **Hover**: Gentle lift (2px), soft shadow, 200ms transition

### Cards
- **Background**: Warm white with subtle wool texture
- **Border**: None, relying on soft shadow
- **Shadow**: 0 4px 20px rgba(212, 200, 188, 0.15)
- **Radius**: 8px for gentle, natural feel

### Forms
- **Inputs**: Oat background, warm charcoal text
- **Focus**: Sage green border, subtle glow
- **Labels**: Suisse Int'l Medium, soft taupe color

## Imagery Style

### Photography Direction
- **Lighting**: Soft, diffused natural light
- **Composition**: Clean, minimalist with generous negative space
- **Color Grading**: Low saturation, warm tones, consistent with palette
- **Mood**: Calm, premium, authentic

### Product Images
- **Background**: Seamless warm white or subtle wool texture
- **Angles**: Multiple views emphasizing texture and craftsmanship
- **Details**: Close-ups of wool fiber, stitching, natural materials
- **Lifestyle**: Urban environments with natural lighting

## Interactive Elements

### Shopping Cart
- **Icon**: Minimalist design in warm charcoal
- **Badge**: Sage green background, cream text
- **Drawer**: Slides from right, oat background with soft shadow

### Product Filters
- **Style**: Clean toggle buttons in oat and sage green
- **Animation**: Smooth transitions with stagger effects
- **State**: Clear visual feedback for selected options

### Navigation
- **Style**: Clean horizontal layout with generous spacing
- **Hover**: Gentle underline animation in sage green
- **Active**: Warm charcoal text with subtle background

This design system ensures every element reinforces the brand's core message of natural luxury meeting urban sophistication, creating a cohesive and emotionally resonant shopping experience.
